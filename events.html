76Th Republic Day 2025

Cheif Guest

Indonesian President Prabowo Subianto graced the event as the Chief Guest 2025, with military contingents from the country participating in a march-past.

The Camel contingent of the Border Security Force

BSF0, under the command of Deputy Commandant Manohar Singh Kheechee, followed by Camel Mounted Band of the BSF and NCC Contingent participated in the parade on Kartavya Path

Republic Day parade

The Republic Day parade showcased the military prowess of the country with several contingents marching in unison and discipline.

Indonesian Armed Forces

Indonesian Armed Forces band performed during the 76th Republic Day parade at the Kartavya Path

Eco-friendly toys-mounted

Eco-friendly toys-mounted Andhra Pradesh tableau earned accolades from the audience at the Republic Day parade. Known as Etikoppaka Bommalu, the exquisite wooden toys are rooted in a 400-year-old tradition of craftsmanship.

Kuno cheetahs.

The Madhya Pradesh tableau celebrated the homecoming of Kuno cheetahs. For the past year, the 24 cheetahs at Kuno – 12 adults and 12 cubs – have been housed inside enclosures.

Meteorological .

the Eartha and Sciences tableaux celebrated The India Meteorological Department's 150 years in the parade on Sunday. The IMD is working to introduce critical interventions that will, in the coming decades, take it from merely being a weather forecaster to a weather manager.

Amrit Kalash .

With depictions of 'Samudra Manthan', 'Amrit Kalash' and holy men bathing on the banks of Sangam, the Republic Day tableau of Uttar Pradesh celebrated the ongoing Maha Kumbh in Prayagraj. Billed as one of the biggest gatherings of humanity on earth, Maha Kumbh 2025 began on January 13 and will culminate on February 26.

Swarnim .

The Jharkhand tableau was themed ‘Swarnim Jharkhand’- A Legacy of Heritage and Progress.

Uttarakhand .

The Uttarakhand tableau showcased cultural heritage and adventure sports

Tribal .

Tribal Affairs Ministry tableau on display during the 76th Republic Day parade at Kartavya Path.

Sarath .

Sarath (ICV BMP-II) system on display during the 76th Republic Day parade, at the Kartavya Path

Dimple.

Captain Dimple Singh Bhati became the first woman officer to present salute to president Droupadi Murmu at the Republic Day Parade.She set a world record by becoming the first woman Army officer to present a salute to President on a 12-foot ladder mounted on a moving motorcycle.

Motorcycle .

The Dare Devils, the Motorcycle Rider Display Team of the Indian Army's Corps of Signals, perform during the 76th Republic Day parade.

BM-21 .

BM-21 Agnibaan, a 122mm Multiple Barrel Rocket Launcher, displayed by the 213 Rocket Regiment and led by Captain Manoj Choniyal.

flower

The Central Public Works Department's flower tableau in the commemorated the 75th anniversary of the enactment of the Constitution. The Ashoka Chakra, depicting the "wheel of time", stood at the front of the tableau while the Constitution of India at the back of the Chakra.

Balloons

Balloons being released during the 76th Republic Day celebration in New Delhi.

IAF

The celebrations ended with a fly past by 40 aircraft and helicopters of the IAF
